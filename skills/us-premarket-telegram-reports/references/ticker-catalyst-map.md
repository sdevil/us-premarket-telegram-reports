# Ticker catalyst map

Use this file as a weighting layer, not as a substitute for price/volume confirmation.

## How to use

For any candidate under consideration:

1. Check the general market framework first:
   - market regime
   - sector strength
   - liquidity
   - relative volume
   - clear trigger levels
2. Then apply ticker-specific weighting.
3. Do not force every ticker through every signal source.
4. Prefer the highest-signal source for that ticker.
5. Treat Trump/policy-style posts as macro or sector signals unless they clearly target a specific company.

## Macro / cross-market special sources

### Trump / policy / campaign-style posts
Use as:
- macro risk sentiment signal
- tariff / trade / China exposure signal
- sector policy signal
- index volatility / futures tone signal

Most relevant when evaluating:
- industrials with tariff sensitivity
- semis with China/export sensitivity
- EV / clean energy / oil & gas / defense / pharma / megacap tech under policy pressure

Do NOT treat this as a direct single-stock catalyst unless the post clearly targets that company.

### Fed / rates / macro policy
Use as:
- market regime signal
- duration / growth sensitivity filter
- bank / real estate / tech multiple sensitivity filter

## Ticker-specific weighting

### TSLA
Priority signals:
- Elon Musk posts / statements
- Tesla official posts or announcements
- delivery data
- FSD / robotaxi / autonomy updates
- China pricing / China delivery / production news
- analyst changes only if tied to a fresh operational catalyst

Interpretation:
- Musk-driven headlines can move the stock fast, but are noisy; require price/volume confirmation.
- For TSLA, social posts matter more than for most large caps.

### AAPL
Priority signals:
- product launch / event announcements
- supply-chain reports
- China demand / iPhone sales commentary
- services growth / App Store / hardware cycle news
- major analyst revisions tied to product cycle or demand

Interpretation:
- Treat conference/product-cycle news as higher signal than generic market commentary.

### NVDA
Priority signals:
- AI conference / keynote / GTC-style event news
- hyperscaler capex updates
- export restrictions / policy actions
- major customer demand or supply commentary
- analyst actions only if tied to fresh AI demand data

Interpretation:
- Strong narrative stock, but still require confirmation from volume and tradability.

### AMD
Priority signals:
- AI accelerator product news
- hyperscaler / enterprise adoption
- competitive positioning versus NVDA
- analyst actions tied to data-center/AI demand

### AVGO
Priority signals:
- AI networking / custom silicon demand
- hyperscaler spend
- VMware / infrastructure software integration news
- major enterprise or AI infrastructure commentary

### MU
Priority signals:
- memory pricing / HBM demand
- AI server buildout implications
- supply-demand commentary
- earnings/guidance tied to memory cycle

### SMCI
Priority signals:
- AI server demand
- customer concentration / order flow
- accounting / governance / filing issues if any
- large partner ecosystem news

Interpretation:
- Event sensitivity is high; governance/accounting headlines can override bullish sector tone.

### MSFT
Priority signals:
- Azure growth / enterprise AI monetization
- OpenAI-related changes only if commercially material
- large enterprise product announcements
- cloud / capex commentary

### AMZN
Priority signals:
- AWS demand / guidance
- consumer / retail trends during key seasons
- fulfillment / margin commentary
- large AI or cloud infrastructure developments

### GOOGL
Priority signals:
- search / ads / cloud updates
- antitrust / regulatory developments
- Gemini / AI monetization with commercial implications

### META
Priority signals:
- ad demand trends
- AI product monetization
- engagement / monetization updates
- regulatory headlines if they affect business model or targeting

### ORCL
Priority signals:
- cloud backlog / cloud growth
- AI infrastructure demand
- large enterprise contracts
- guidance revisions

### NFLX
Priority signals:
- subscriber / engagement / ad-tier metrics
- content slate catalysts
- pricing changes
- ad monetization updates

### Biotech / pharma examples (LLY, NVO, VRTX, REGN)
Priority signals:
- clinical trial results
- FDA / regulatory updates
- label expansion / reimbursement / safety issues
- major conference data releases

Interpretation:
- Trial/FDA news should outrank generic sector strength by a wide margin.

## Output behavior rules

When a candidate belongs to one of the names above:
- mention the most relevant ticker-specific driver in the thesis when applicable
- prefer that driver over generic sector commentary
- explicitly downgrade a setup if the stock lacks its usual high-signal catalyst

When a macro special source is active:
- state whether it affects the market, sector, or specific ticker
- adjust risk level and candidate ranking accordingly
- do not overfit a broad political/social post into a fake single-stock thesis
